Best Egg does not run a public, self-serve affiliate portal. They distribute
through networks (historically CJ Affiliate; rumored on Impact at various
points) and through bespoke direct partnerships (e.g. SuperMoney). For a
pre-traffic site like apruvee.com, the realistic Tier-1 options in priority
order are:

1. **Direct partnership outreach** to Best Egg / Marlette Funding (this packet).
2. **CJ Affiliate** — already covered by the separate task
   `apply-cj-affiliate.md`. Best Egg is the strongest Tier-1 target inside CJ.
   Apply via CJ in parallel; whichever approves first is the channel of record.
3. **Pepperjam / Ascend** — Best Egg has appeared there historically; it is
   covered by the separate `apply-pepperjam.md` task. Same parallel logic
   applies.

**Do not double-promote.** If Best Egg approves you on more than one channel,
pick one (typically the one with the highest CPL/CPF and best reporting) and
formally decline or pause the others. Running two trackers against the same
advertiser is the fastest way to get banned for "duplicate publisher."

## Channels to use (in send order)

### Channel 1 — LinkedIn (primary, highest signal)

LinkedIn DM/InMail is the most reliable way to reach a real human in a
fintech partnerships team. The lender's verified company page is:

- **Best Egg:** https://www.linkedin.com/company/bestegg/

Steps:

1. From that company page, filter People → search keywords:
   `affiliate`, `partnerships`, `business development`, `growth marketing`.
2. Identify **2 people maximum**. Look for titles like "Director of
   Partnerships", "Affiliate Marketing Manager", "Head of Growth", or
   "Business Development". Prefer someone who has been at the company > 12
   months and lists Wilmington, DE / Philadelphia / NYC.
3. Send a connection request with a 300-character personalized note (template
   below). Do **not** mass-spam multiple people in the same week — fintech
   partnerships teams talk to each other; one focused, well-written ask wins.
4. If they accept, follow up via DM with the longer email body.
5. If no response in 7 business days, send InMail (paid) — same body, slightly
   shorter open.

### Channel 2 — Email (parallel)

Best Egg does not publish a partnerships address. Send the email to **all**
of the following on the same thread; the ones that bounce will fail fast and
the live one(s) will route internally:

- `partnerships@bestegg.com` — most likely live
- `affiliates@bestegg.com` — second most likely
- `marketing@bestegg.com` — fallback
- `info@marlettefunding.com` — corporate fallback at parent

> **Do not** invent a named contact. Several AI summaries online list a
> "Lisa Albiston" at `lisaalbiston@bestegg.com` as Head of Corporate Affairs.
> That contact is **not verified from a primary source** in the research for
> this packet — sending to an unverified named address risks being filtered as
> spam or, worse, looking sloppy to a real person who reads it. Use the
> generic addresses above instead, and let the receiving team route it.

### Channel 3 — Contact form (last resort)

`https://www.bestegg.com/contact-us/` has only customer-care contacts. If
nothing else lands, submit the partnerships pitch through the customer-care
form and ask them to forward to the partnerships team. Low yield, but
non-zero.

## Likely outcomes

- **No reply (most likely first pass).** Send the LinkedIn note, wait 7
  business days, send the InMail/email follow-up, wait another 7. If still
  silent, fall back to the CJ path and mark this task as "Closed — no
  response, deferred to CJ" until apruvee.com has live traffic to lead with.
- **"Apply via [network]" reply.** Common — Best Egg will often route new
  publishers to CJ even if a direct program technically exists. That's a
  win, not a loss; the email got read and you now have a known channel.
- **Approved direct.** Less likely pre-traffic but not impossible given the
  niche fit. If it happens, treat it as anchor-tenant news for the launch
  press / homepage.
- **Declined for traffic reasons.** Standard. Ask for the specific
  threshold in writing, log it here, and reapply at that traffic level.
